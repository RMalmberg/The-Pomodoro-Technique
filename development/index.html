About

Study-Plan-Organize was created for those who enjoy studying with the methodology provided by the Pomodoro Technique®.

What is Pomodoro?

The Pomodoro Technique® is based on the Pomodoro theory of productivity, created by Francesco Cirillo, where 25 minutes of work is balanced by a following 5 minutes of rest. After the fourth interval of 25 minutes, a 15 minute break is taken. As it stands, this timer measures the 25 minute work period.

Benefits of Pomodoro

- Manage distractions & control your time

- Increase accountability

- Improve weekly and quarterly planning

- Maintain motivation!
